Our Story

2017

The firm was founded on 01 January 2017. The firm name, TZEDEK, embodies the Biblical meaning of "justice" and "righteousness", and we strive to achieve this in all our dealings.

Our Specialization

We are a boutique conveyancing law firm in Singapore providing quality service to its clients. We are a team of forward-thinking professionals specialising in retail conveyancing and property-related matters.

Our Expertise

The primary specialisation of our advocate(s) and solicitor(s) in the firm is conveyancing but there is a good balance and mix of experience in other areas of law such as criminal, general litigation, arbitration, matrimonial, estate planning, etc.

Our Achievements

Our work is a combination of excellence, honesty with integrity. We offer effective solutions, a direct line to our Managing Director, and an assurance of quality work for both our institutional and private clients.
