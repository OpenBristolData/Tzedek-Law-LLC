Ancillary Services

Drafting of Wills

Ensure your assets are distributed according to your wishes with our professional will drafting services.

Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA)

Plan for the future by appointing someone you trust to make decisions on your behalf should you lose mental capacity.

Power of Attorney (POA)

Authorize someone to act on your behalf for specific matters or during specific periods.

Grant of Probate

A grant of probate is a legal document issued by the court that confirms the validity of a will and grants the executor the authority to manage and distribute the deceased's estate according to the will's provisions.

Letter of Administration

Letters of administration are issued by the probate registry and grant a family member or friend the authority to act as the administrator of a deceased person's estate when there is no valid will.

Deed Poll

A deed poll is a legal document that declares your new name and renounces your old name. We provide professional deed poll services for official name changes.
